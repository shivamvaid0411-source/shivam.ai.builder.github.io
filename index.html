The process

From agreement
to live — in 72 hours

01

We agree on scope

One call or email exchange. I confirm your platform (WordPress, Shopify, custom HTML, etc.), colors, and any specific needs like languages or product categories.

02

I build and customize

I train the bot on your website — all your pages, products, brand voice, and navigation. I match your brand colors exactly. You get a demo link to review before anything goes live.

03

Integration — one snippet

I paste one <script> tag into your website. For WordPress: I use a plugin, no developer needed. For Shopify: I edit the theme file. For GTM: I create a tag — takes 5 minutes.

04

Live & maintained

Your chatbot goes live. Every month I update it when you add new collections or pages. You pay a small monthly fee — or a one-time setup if you prefer to manage it yourself.
